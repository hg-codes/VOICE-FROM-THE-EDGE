The Chenchu Kinnera

An ancient, handcrafted stringed instrument native to Telangana. Historically played by the Chenchu and Dakkali tribes, it is used to recount epic ballads and heroic tales.

Unlike modern factory instruments, the Kinnera is built entirely from the forest — bamboo, dried bottle gourds, animal horns, and honey-wax.
